The collapsible panel appears either directly below the primary navigation or the page header, depending on the panel's content. The panel can show up on either the left or the right side of the page.

As a general rule, navigation, filters, and drag & drop functionality should appear on the left side of the page. Right side panels should appear collapsed on page load.
